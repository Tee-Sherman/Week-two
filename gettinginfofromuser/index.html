About us

In 2014, we founded our first store in Frankfort, Illinois. We set out to bring a higher quality and consistency to the industry than what was currently available at the time, and we have definitely succeeded! Through our intense employee training, excellent customer service and education, fair pricing, and constant quest to find the newest and best products available, we have easily become the most trusted name in the industry. Since then, we have expanded into 10 stores and grow every single day with the loyalty of our very satisfied customers, and have helped thousands of people quit smoking cigarettes by providing healthier alternatives. Our stores are proud to boost the largest selection of vaping products around, from pens, juices, pods, and complete kits. We have the largest volume of products for nicotine and nicotine free vaping-whether you’re trying to quit smoking or are a vaping hobbyist, we have what you need! We’re also happy to provide the finest curated selection of glass pipes, vaporizers, kits, CBD and tobacco related items, candles, incense, tapestries, glass art, jewelry, and seasonal items; so come on in and you’ll be happy too!
